Sandwhiches & Wraps

Vegan Hummas Wrap Vietnamese Summer Rolls with Chicken$ Grilled Chicken Sandwich

    For the Summer Rolls:

  1. 1 cup thin rice vermicelli noodles
  2. 2 tablespoons olive oil
  3. 6 (8.5 inch) rice wrappers
  4. 1 medium cucumber, julienned
  5. 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  6. 1 medium carrot, julienned
  7. ¼ cup fresh basil or cilantro, chopped
  8. pinch of salt
  9. 1 cup chicken breast, cooked

    10. For the Sauce:

  10. 2 tablespoons hoisin sauce (or black bean sauce)
  11. 1 tablespoon peanut butter
  12. 1 tablespoon water
  13. ½ tablespoon fresh lime juice
  14. 1-2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
  15. 1 tablespoon roasted peanuts, chopped
  16. pinch of salt

    *These are perfect for those in a time crunch!

  1. 3 tbsp hummus
  2. tortilla wrap, warmed
  3. cucummbers
  4. tomato
  5. avacado
  6. spinach
  7. mixed greens
  8. 1/4 tsp sea salt
  9. pinch of black pepper
  10. Method:
    • Lightly warm tortilla shell.
    • Spread on hummus. Spread the hummus into a long rectangular shape down the centre of the tortilla wrap.
    • Add veggies. Layer the sliced cucumber, tomato, and avocado on top.
    • Add greens. Top up with spinach and mixed greens.
    • Add seasoning. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Wrap it up. Wrap the tortilla into a burrito shape and fold in the sides. Cut it in the middle and serve.
