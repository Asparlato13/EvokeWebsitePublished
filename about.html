A little about me..

Greetings! I'm Adrianna Parlato, a 22-year-old from Buffalo, NY. I recently graduated from Loyola Maryland University, where I pursued a major in Information Systems and a minor in Data Science. Combining my main passions for health, wellness, and technology, I've embarked on a mission with Evoke, centered around the idea of 'Know Thyself.'

Evoke serves as the culmination of years of research and personal discoveries, all gathered and shared with the hope of making a positive impact on others. Even if just one person benefits from the information I provide, it will be a success. Through this platform, I have the opportunity to develop my coding, writing, and creative skills, while also offering practical advice and insights that have helped me on my own journey towards self-improvement.

Join me as I lay out effective habits and valuable pointers that have empowered me to become the best version of myself. Together, we can embark on a transformative journey toward self-discovery and personal growth.
