Granola

  1. 1.5 cups rolled oats (look for gluten-free variety)
  2. 3/4 cup hazelnuts, chopped
  3. 3/4 cup pecans, chopped
  4. 1 tsp cinnamon
  5. 1/2 cup shredded coconut
  6. 2 tbsp melted coconut oil
  7. 1/3 cup cacao nibs
  8. 1/4 cup Cacao Powder
  9. pinch of salt
  10. 1/2 cup rice malt syrup
  11. 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  12. pinch of salt
  13. Method:
    • Preheat the oven to 180*C. Line a tray with non-stick baking paper and set aside.
    • Combine oats, nuts & seeds, coconut, protein, cinnamon and salt into a bowl. Mix well.
    • Spread onto the baking tray in an even layer and bake for 25-30 minutes or until golden, giving it a stir at the halfway mark.
    • Cool for 10 minutes before giving the granola another stir. Cool completely and transfer into an air-tight jar or container. Store at room temperature for up to 1 month.

  7. 3/4 cup plant-based milk, warmed & frothed
  1. 1/4 cup Tropeaka Protect Herbal Infusion Tea
  2. 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  3. Zest of half an orange
  4. Juice of 1 orange
  5. 1/4 cup fresh mint leaves
  6. Maple syrup, to taste
  7. 1/2 tbsp Tropeaka Immunity Powder
  8. Method:
    • Steep the herbal infusion in 750mL (3 cups) boiling water for 10 minutes, or longer depending on how strong you want it to be.
    • Pour in the tea along with 375mL (1.5 cups) of room temperature water. Sweeten to taste with maple syrup and enjoy while warm. Alternately, chill in the fridge before serving.
